# Code of Conduct

We are committed to a friendly, safe, and welcoming community.

## Be respectful
- Assume good intent, be constructive, and keep feedback actionable.
- No harassment, hate speech, or personal attacks.

## Collaborate
- Listen, ask questions, and help others learn the codebase.
- Celebrate contributions of all sizes: docs, tests, and bugfixes count.

## Report issues
- If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please let the maintainers know via the security/contact channel in `SECURITY.md`.

## Enforcement
- Maintainers may warn, limit participation, or remove contributors who violate this policy.
